About Me

My grandma always used to say that every person has its own way of preparing food. If you give a few people exaclty same instructions and ingredients, final result will significantly differ from each other. They all can be tasty, but one might feel difference. This is because everyone who loves cooking has their own way of doing things.

I started my career journey as a math and computer science teacher in public school, then worked in a telecommunication company as a software tester and all this time I always loved to cook. Later I understand that this passion has been inherited from my grandma - Professional Chef. I got lucky that I could follow my passion and continue my career in that direction. Once refined with formal culinary training, working in professional environment, took me to a whole new level of what was once a hobby. Baking and cake decoration now become much more than a hobbby.

Currently, I'm a food blogger, vlogger, food photographer and the brightest point of my career from now is passing this experience to the next generation.
